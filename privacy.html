Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Phoenix Business System (“PBS”), operated through Rising Phoenix Enterprises, collects, uses, stores, and protects information submitted through PBS surfaces, pages, forms, assessments, execution layers, and related service pathways.

By using PBS, you acknowledge this policy and consent to the handling of information as described here.
Information Collection

1. Information We May Collect

Depending on the PBS surface used, we may collect information you choose to provide, including:

  • name, email address, phone number, and other contact details,
  • business information, operational details, responses, and intake data,
  • uploaded materials, summaries, declarations, or execution-related submissions,
  • billing and transaction-related information processed through approved payment providers,
  • communications or support-related information you voluntarily provide.
Use of Information

2. How Information Is Used

We may use submitted information to:

  • deliver PBS products, pathways, structured outputs, and service access,
  • administer user progression, execution acknowledgment, or governed service states,
  • process transactions and maintain service continuity,
  • respond to inquiries, support requests, or operational contact needs,
  • improve clarity, continuity, administration, and quality of PBS services.
Data Position

3. Limited Use Principle

We collect and use information primarily for service delivery, structured PBS administration, transaction continuity, and related operational purposes.

We do not state that user information is sold as a business practice. Data use is directed toward service functionality, service administration, lawful business operation, and continuity of the PBS experience.

Payments

4. Payment Information

Payments may be processed through approved third-party payment processors. We do not necessarily store full payment card details directly on PBS pages. Payment-related information may be handled according to the policies and security practices of the processor used.

Uploads and Submissions

5. Uploaded Materials and Execution Submissions

If a PBS surface allows documents, summaries, declarations, screenshots, or other client-submitted materials, those materials may be used to support service delivery, execution acknowledgment, system administration, or related continuity functions within the applicable PBS pathway.

Users remain responsible for ensuring that submitted materials are lawful, accurate, appropriate, and authorized for submission.

Retention

6. Data Retention

We may retain submitted information, service records, transaction records, acknowledgment records, and related materials for as long as reasonably necessary to operate PBS, maintain continuity, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, protect system integrity, or preserve service history.

Protection

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures intended to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet-based system, transmission method, or digital storage environment can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Third Parties

8. Third-Party Services

PBS may rely on third-party services for hosting, communication, analytics, payment processing, delivery support, or related operations. Where such services are used, information may be processed through those providers to support PBS functionality and lawful business operations.

User Choice

9. Your Use of PBS

You may choose not to provide certain information, but doing so may limit access to specific PBS surfaces, outputs, pathways, purchases, or governed service functions.

Policy Updates

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be revised from time to time to reflect operational, legal, structural, or service changes. Continued use of PBS after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy as posted on this page.
